Value Proposition

The Get A Bar difference

The only self-service premium cocktail bar in Nigeria that guarantees identical taste, verified ingredients, and pure-water ice at every private event — without a bartender army.

  • Machine precision: every cocktail identical, every time — no improvisation
  • Ingredient transparency: guests see spirits, choose pods, control strength (Mocktail → Strong)
  • Pure-water ice standard: bottled or filtered water only — a premium signal nobody else makes
  • Guest autonomy: self-service model removes the staffing intrusion from private events
  • 60+ cocktail & mocktail varieties across event themes
  • Muslim & non-drinker inclusive: genuine Mocktail mode for every setup
  • Discreet coordination: one elegant attendant; privacy preserved
  • Occasion-curated pod menus: themed selections per event type
