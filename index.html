01   The Problem

The cocktail industry has a consistency crisis.

Walk into any private event in Lagos. Someone ordered a cocktail service. The drinks look the part — but halfway through the night, the same cocktail tastes different. The ice has melted in the heat. Nobody really knows which alcohol went into the third round. And the bartender? Halfway across the room.

The people paying premium prices for event bar services deserve better than improvised mixology and guesswork. They deserve precision — and they deserve privacy.

⚖
Taste Inconsistency

Manual cocktail mixing varies from drink to drink, bartender to bartender. The third glass never tastes like the first.

❓
Quality Opacity

Guests never truly know what spirit — and at what strength — went into their drink. Premium pricing, uncertain quality.

🧊
The Ice Problem

Standard ice uses unfiltered tap water, affects flavour, dilutes drinks unpredictably. Nobody talks about it. Everyone notices it.

🚪
Privacy at Private Events

High-net-worth hosts don't want strangers flowing through their homes. A bar team of five is an intrusion, not a service.

02   The Concept

The answer isn't a better bartender.
It's a smarter bar.

Get A Bar brings precision cocktail machine technology — led by Bartesian, the world's most sophisticated home and commercial cocktail maker — into the premium private events space in Nigeria. Every drink, identical. Every ingredient, verified. Every guest, empowered.

🎯

Machine Precision

Bartesian's cocktail makers use pod-based barcoded capsules — each scanned and dispensed to an exact recipe. The machine never improvises. Every Margarita at 11pm is identical to the first one served at 7pm.

Consistency guaranteed
✅

Total Transparency

Guests choose their cocktail capsule. They see the spirit bottles. They select their strength — from Mocktail to Strong. What goes in is visible. What comes out is predictable. No mysteries.

Every ingredient, known
💧

Pure Ice Standard

Get A Bar's ice makers are fed exclusively from premium bottled or filtered water — never tap. The ice in your guest's glass meets the same standard as the spirit. A detail nobody else in Lagos is talking about.

Purity, not compromise
🔑

Self-Service Freedom

The event host's guests control their own experience. Walk up. Choose a capsule. Pick strength. Press mix. Receive a perfectly crafted cocktail in under 30 seconds. No queueing. No waiting. No awkward requests.

Empowered guests
🎭

Variety by Design

60+ cocktail varieties across multiple Bartesian machines, themed to any event — intimate birthday dinner, corporate gala, bridal shower, cultural celebration. The pod selection can be curated for each occasion.

Themed to any event
🤫

Discreet Coordination

One elegantly presented coordinator attends — not to serve, but to oversee. Machine levels maintained. Capsule selection refreshed. Ice topped up. Presence minimal. Experience seamless.

Privacy preserved

03   Brand Story

The story of Get A Bar

Brand Name Get A Bar
Brand Promise Every drink, exactly as intended. Every time.
Brand Personality Confident. Precise. Quietly luxurious. A little bit dramatic.
Primary Market Lagos high-net-worth private events; corporate launches; premium social occasions
Technology Partner Bartesian (USA) — commercial-grade cocktail machines trusted by hotels, stadiums and cruise lines

There is a moment at every great private event — when a perfectly made drink arrives in the right hand, at the right moment — and the room shifts. The conversation deepens. The laughter gets louder. The evening becomes a memory.

Get A Bar was built around that moment. Not around the bar itself — around what it enables.

The founders of Get A Bar spent years observing something that nobody in Nigeria's event industry was talking about: the more premium the event, the more the drink service was letting it down. Inconsistent cocktails. Mystery ingredients. Ice from questionable water. Five strangers in a private home, more intrusive than celebratory.

They asked: what if the bar could run itself?

Not the cheap version — the luxury version. A Bartesian Professional cocktail machine that makes 60 bar-quality cocktails at the push of a button. Spirit bottles you can see. Pod capsules with barcodes that ensure the machine never uses the wrong alcohol. Strength settings from Mocktail to Strong, so every guest — Muslim or not, drinking or abstaining — finds their perfect drink. Ice made from bottled water, for flavour purity that most establishments don't even consider.

"We didn't want to be another cocktail service. We wanted to be the first cocktail standard. The bar that's always correct — because it was never improvising."

The name came naturally: Get A Bar. Direct. Confident. A command as much as an invitation. It reflects how the founders see their clients — decisive people who know what they want and choose the best available option to get it. Get A Bar is not for everyone. It is for the host who cares about the details their guests notice but would never mention. The ones who know the difference between a good night and a great one is often invisible — a consistent drink, a clean glass, a service that disappears into the evening rather than demanding attention from it.

Get A Bar is built on a simple belief: luxury is not about excess. It is about precision. And precision — at every glass, at every pour, for every guest — is what Get A Bar delivers.

06   Revenue Streams

Seven ways Get A Bar earns

01

Machine & Setup Rental

Core revenue: tiered rental fee per event covering machine deployment, setup, and takedown.

02

Pod & Spirit Package

Cocktail pod capsules and spirit bottles sold as a bundled package per event, marked up from cost.

03

Glassware Premium

Branded glassware upgrade over standard cups — a per-head or per-event charge for exquisite glass service.

04

Mixologist Service

Add-on human flair: a certified mixologist to demo, engage guests, and add panache. Charged per hour or per event.

05

Corporate Retainer

Monthly or quarterly agreements with corporate clients for recurring event deployments at a preferred rate.

06

Dry Hire (Machine Only)

Machine-only rental for clients who self-manage their event. Lower ASP, higher volume, minimal overhead.

07

Brand Partnerships

Spirit brand co-branding at high-profile events; sponsored pod capsule sets for product launches and activations.

08

Custom Pod Curation

Themed pod menus designed for specific events — weddings, cultural celebrations, brand launches — at a curation fee.
